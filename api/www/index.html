Ratings & more!

Notflix is an online rating platform for movies. This platform is meant for you and your friends to share the rating of a certain movie with each other. If you and your friends are couch potatoes and addicted to movies; do not hestitate and register now!

Movies

All the movies that are added to this platform can be rated. You can simply modify or delete your own rating, and see the average of all ratings together. All the details are substracted from the official IMDB website.

Users

In order to place ratings, you must be logged in. Everyone can register for this platform and so place ratings.
